# Brand Reference — Weissman Foundry

Distilled from the Foundry brand guidelines and the diamond-fold handout. Use these
when producing real artwork. The mockups follow these values.

## Palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Deep Green | `#0D5B3E` | Primary surface / panels |
| Forest Dark | `#063D2A` | Footers, deep contrast, text on light |
| Foundry Gold | `#F2B417` | Primary accent, bands, CTAs, the "yellow apron" |
| Teal | `#6CC5C0` | Secondary — collaborative spaces |
| Sage | `#7FA653` | Secondary — materials & stock |
| Paper | `#F7F4EC` | Warm light background |
| Ink | `#0A261B` | Body text |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Wordmark/type on green |

Avoid Babson's institutional kelly green — Foundry green is deeper and warmer.

### Wayfinding color key
- **Gold** = Fabrication studios
- **Teal** = Collaborative spaces
- **Sage** = Materials & stock

## Type

- **Archivo** family only.
- Headlines: **Archivo Black (900)**, UPPERCASE, tight tracking (~-2%).
- Body: Regular / Medium, sentence case. **Never set body in all caps.**

## Wordmark

- Real vector in `brand/wordmark.svg` (white strokes/fills as exported, for dark bg).
- `brand/wordmark_green.svg` (#0D5B3E) for light backgrounds.
- `brand/wordmark_white.svg` (#FFFFFF) for green/dark backgrounds.
- The three overlapping shapes in the Foundry mark (square/circle/triangle) read as
  "AND" — collaborative *and* experimental *and* future-facing — and nod to the three
  colleges. Don't label which is which.

## Geometric pattern

- `brand/Foundry_Pattern.jpg` — a modular grid of square tiles, each holding one motif
  (full/partial circles, squares, nested squares, four-rotation triangles, chevrons,
  diagonal stripe fans) in the palette above.
- **Rules:** keep tiles square (never stretch). Use it **contained** — a header strip,
  a footer strip, a diamond accent — not scattered. Brand guide: "if it looks like
  confetti, pull back." In the mockups it's reconstructed as SVG; swap for crops of the
  real JPG in production.

## Icons

- Single line-icon family, ~2.2 px stroke, rounded joins (woodshop, laser, 3D print,
  textiles, etc.). The mockup icons are in this spirit but are reconstructions —
  prefer official Foundry icons if available.

## Voice

- Encouraging, direct, a little playful. "Talk like a Scout, not a brochure."
- It's a **launchpad / prototyping hub**, not a craft studio.
- Active phrasing: "Bring your idea to life" (the shirt line) over "What will you create?"
- Handout beats to echo: "Where your ideas become reality," "Go beyond the expected,"
  "From concept to product," "All ideas welcome."
